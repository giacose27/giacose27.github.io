Vibes

Branding & Packaging

Project Overview

The goal of this project was to create branding and packaging for a fictional canned cocktail brand named Vibes. They were looking for a visual identity that is bold, punchy, and premium. They provide bar-quality drinks to those who are bold enough to try something new.

Research and Specifications

I began this project by researching canned cocktail brands and flavors.

First Draft

For the first draft, I started by creating my color palette using color psycology. Purple for premium feel, orange and yellow for fun vibes. Then, I made the logo and logo variations and began looking for mockup templates.

Work in Progress

During the project process, I tried different color schemes and layouts for the cans. I also created the slogan and nutrition label in this stage.

Obstacles and Solutions

It was a challenge to keep the brand colors prominent on the cans while still using the relevent colors for each flavor. As I tried out different color combinations and designs, many of the styles I used caused the flavor colors to clash with the brand colors. To fix this problem, I created a color blocked style with the logo and brand colors in the center, and the flavor colors on the rest of the can.

Critique and Revisions

The critique I recieved on this project was to bring the letters on the logo closer together to connect them. To implement this, I outlined the type and moved the letters into a better alignment by hand.

Next Steps

At this part in the project, I designed the six pack packaging using the same style as the cans. To finalize this branding, I created mockups of the packaging and a tote bag.

Final Product and Mockups

The final products are branding and packaging for a bold and premium brand that's all about bringing the good vibes.
