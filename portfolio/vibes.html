Vibes

Branding & Packaging

Project Overview

The goal of this project was to create branding and packaging for a fictional canned cocktail brand named Vibes. They were looking for a visual identity that is bold, punchy, and premium. They provide bar-quality drinks to those who are bold enough to try something new.

Research and Specifications

I began this project by researching canned cocktail brands and flavors.

First Draft

From there, I designed the logo variations and began looking for mockup templates.

Work in Progress

Once I had my logos and mockup templates, I started creating a pattern that would be used on the boxes and other items. I wanted to keep the patten simple, so I just used the "P" from the logo. I made both black and white versions of the pattern to match the logo variants.

Obstacles and Solutions

It was a challenge to keep the brand colors prominent on the cans while still using the relevent colors for each flavor. As I tried out different color combinations and designs, many of the styles I used caused the flavor colors to clash with the brand colors. To fix this problem, I created a color blocked style with the logo and brand colors in the center, and the flavor colors on the rest of the can.

Critique and Revisions

When I was starting this project, I used a lot of dark colors in my palette. However, as the brand became more fleshed out, I realized that the darkness was overshadowing the freshness and brightness of the brand. This was the point in the project where I realized I needed to add more white and light green.
