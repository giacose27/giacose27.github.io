Daily Yolk

Menu Card & Branding

Project Overview

The goal of this project was to create branding and a menu card for a fictional all-day breakfast club called Daily Yolk. They were looking for a brand identity that is cozy, modern, playful and warm.

Research and Specifications

I began this project by researching breakfast clubs and menu cards. Then, I chose the fonts, colors, and designed the logo.

First Draft

From there, I branched out and created a small coffee menu tent design for the tables.

Work in Progress

I started looking for the mockups that I would use for the coffee cups and menus. During this process I found more ideas for brand assets I could create.

Obstacles and Solutions

Choosing the typography for Daily Yolk was a major obstacle during this project. Once I chose the font for the logo, pairing it with a contrasting subheader font became tricky. I searched through my fonts and eventually decided to go with Nitti Typewriter. I think this font fits the vibe of the brand perfectly.

Critique and Revisions

When I asked for critique on this project, it was recommended that I make the words in the menu subheaders detached and fluid, with an egg yolk background. I implemented those ideas and made alternating egg yolk and maple syrup styles.

Next Steps

To finalize this branding, I created mockups of all the brand assets I created and designed an Instagram post.

Final Product and Mockups

The final product is a fun, modern brand that includes various logo styles, coffee cup designs, a large menu and mini cafe tabletop menu! The logo designs have many uses, with just one example being a paper holder for a coffee cup.
