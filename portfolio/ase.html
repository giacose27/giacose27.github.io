Andrew Sumbillo Entertainment

Branding & Logos

Project Overview

The goal of this project was to create branding and logo designs for Andrew Sumbillo Entertainment. ASE is a booking agency for bands, duos, djs, and musicians. This brand was designed to have a visual identity that is fun, fast-paced, reliable, and trustworthy.


Research and Specifications

For this project, I researched different booking agencies and came up with a color palette that matched the energy of the brand. Many of the booking agencies used dark blues and purples and bold black text. I decided to keep that in mind when creating the logo and color palette for ASE, taking inspiration from them and also moving away from the same styles to make this brand stand out.

First Draft

From there, I began looking for font styles that encompass the boldness and fun of this brand. I made sure to keep the name “Andrew Sumbillo” the main element of the logo. I also tested out the different color combinations that could be used for the logo based on the color palette.

Work in Progress

Once I had the standard logo, I created a few variations in color and shape. Then I began creating mockups of the logo.

Obstacles and Solutions

The most difficult part of this project was designing the brand to feel both business-causal and fun. I reconciled this by using contrasting fonts and colors that combine the energy of a live performance and corporate.

Critique and Revisions

When I asked for critique on this project, the advice given to me was to move the word “Entertainment” down slightly to make the name easier to read.

Next Steps

Once I was finished with this logo and branding, I created a short animation of the standard logo in After Effects.

Final Product and Mockups

The final products include branding, five logo variants, and one logo animation for Andrew Sumbillo Entertainment.
