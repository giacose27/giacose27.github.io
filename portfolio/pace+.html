Pace+

Packaging & Branding

Project Overview

The goal of this project was to create branding and packaging for Pace+. Pace+ is a fictional skincare brand made for athletes to protect their skin during workouts and active days. They wanted a brand identity that clean, modern, and active.

Research and Specifications

For this project, I researched different athletic skincare brands. I found that they all had different looks and vibes but none of them looked like athletic brands. I decided that I would design Pace+ to stand out as an brand for athletes.

First Draft

From there, I started creating the color palette for Pace+. I decided to use mostly neutrals with pops of blue and orange to really lean into the clean athletic vibe of this brand.

Work in Progress

When choosing the font for the logo, I looked for something that was clean, bold, and italicized. I decided to add both the blue and orange to the plus sign to add visual intrigue and make good use of the color palette.

Obstacles and Solutions

The most difficult part of this project was designing the layout for the product labels. Designing a brand that is clean and minimal that still stands out on a shelf can be tricky. To overcome this challenge, I used simple imagery that adds visual interest while reinforcing the clean and athletic look.

Critique and Revisions

Originally, the back of the bottles were plain with the exception of the text. To make the design look more complete, I added a low opacity logo pattern in the background.

Next Steps

When I made the mockups, I decided to create another pattern with both the blue and orange plus signs to be used as a transparent packaging tape design.

Final Product and Mockups

The final products include four product label designs, two logos, and one box and packaging tape design.
