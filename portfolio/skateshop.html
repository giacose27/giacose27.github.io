Skate Shop

UX/UI Conceptual Website & Logo

Project Overview

The goal of this project was to wireframe and design a website for a fictional skate shop. This project was made for F.I.R.S.T. Institute.



Before and After

Research and Specifications

Since the old version of this website was based more on skateboarding shops, I wanted to lean more into the rollerskating side for this rework. I knew that I wanted to give this website a Y2K vibe. So, I began by researching the Y2K aesthetic and making a moodboard in FigJam.

First Draft

From there, I brought my moodboard and color palette into Figma and began creating the frames and wireframes. During this stage, I also created the logo. The logo from the original version of this project was provided by F.I.R.S.T. Institute, so I decided to make an updated one that was a better fit for this new branding.

Work in Progress

I finished up my Mi-Fi wireframes and started to search for royalty free images on Unsplash.

Obstacles and Solutions

One obstacle I faced when completing this project was creating the holographic effect. Since the technique only works on a simple shape and requires increasing the shape's stroke to fill in the entire space, I had to slightly adjust the style of the logo. Increasing the stroke to fill the entire planet shape caused it to lose it's shape, so I couldn't make the entire logo holographic and only increased the stroke to fill half of the shape instead. By adding a smaller black planet shape in the center, I was able to cover the white space left in the middle.

Critique and Revisions

When I asked for critique on this project, it became clear that the "computer pop-up" style of the homepage was not easily recognizable, as people were telling me that the alignment was off. To fix this UI issue, I added the red, yellow/gray, and green buttons as stylistic elements.

Next Steps

I spent the rest of the time working on the project, filling in text, making buttons, fixing alignment, and prototyping.

Final Product and Mockups

The final product is a simple, Y2K style website concept and logo for the brand Skate Shop.
